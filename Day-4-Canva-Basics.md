# Day-4: Canva Basics - Design Kaise Banaye Free Me

Today I learned 3 things about Canva:

1. Canva free hai - Logo, Poster, Instagram Post sab ban sakta hai
2. Template use karo - 0 se banane ki zarurat nahi, ready design milte hain
3. Drag-Drop hota hai - Photo daalo, Text likho, Download karo

Best line: "Achhi design ke liye Designer nahi, Canva chalana aana chahiye."
